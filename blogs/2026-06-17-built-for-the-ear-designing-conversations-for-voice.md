The most important decision about implementing voice AI comes before conversation design and starts with selecting the right use case, since not every conversation belongs on a phone call. Password resets, order lookups, and interactions ending in links or codes are better suited to chat, while voice AI excels when problems are urgent, complex, or emotionally nuanced, such as patients describing symptoms or customers disputing charges.
